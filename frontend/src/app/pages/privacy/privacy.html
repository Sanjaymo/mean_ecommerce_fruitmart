Privacy Policy

This policy explains how FruitMart collects, processes, and safeguards personal data while you browse, place orders, and contact support.

Last updated: {{ legalLastUpdated }}

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details including name, email, phone, and encrypted login credentials.
  • Order records including items, addresses, payment mode, and delivery status.
  • Support interaction history for order, payment, and quality-related assistance.
  • Basic technical diagnostics used for reliability, fraud prevention, and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver orders accurately and within expected timelines.
  • To send confirmations, tracking updates, and support responses.
  • To improve catalog quality, fulfillment operations, and platform UX.
  • To detect suspicious activity and protect account security.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

FruitMart does not sell personal data. Limited information may be shared with delivery partners, payment processors, and service providers strictly for order fulfillment and support operations.

4. Data Security

We apply layered security controls including restricted access, session safeguards, and secure backend handling. No system is absolutely risk-free, so controls are continuously reviewed and improved.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Update personal profile details from account settings.
  • Request support for order history and account clarifications.
  • Contact us directly for privacy-specific questions or requests.

6. Contact for Privacy Requests

Use the Contact page and include the subject line "Privacy Request" using your registered account email for faster verification.
